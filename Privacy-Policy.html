Privacy Policy for GamyGo

Last Updated:

1. Data Collection

GamyGo does not collect, store, or share any user data. All game-related information (such as progress or preferences) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Internet & Connectivity

The game works completely offline and does not require any internet connection to function. No data is ever transmitted to servers or third parties.

3. Security & Storage

Because GamyGo stores data locally, it remains private and only accessible on your device. If you delete the game, all stored data will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

4. No Third-Party Involvement

GamyGo does not use third-party tools, analytics, ads, or tracking systems. Your data stays completely yours.

5. Legal Compliance

As no personal data is collected or processed, GamyGo complies with all relevant privacy regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and Saudi Data Protection Law.

6. Updates to This Policy

If any future updates change how data is handled, we will notify users inside the app before applying changes.
